FAQs
Questions? Look here.
Why is Success Score not reflecting the business appropriately?
Success Score is a first of its kind comprehensive culmination of universally accepted and followed rating and underwriting principles.The score is based on 6 broad components Financial Management, Financial Standing, Access to Finance, Insurance Risk Management, Stability & Survival and Revenue Potential.It is AI & tech driven, dynamic and covers not only financial parameters but also non-financial parameters therefore giving a comprehensive score.
What is DSCR? Why is it important?
DSCR is a critical parameter used by all financial agencies to quantify future loan perspectives, and liquidity position of businesses, it means Debt Service Coverage Ratio, in simple terms, it gauges No of times Profits generated in the year to debt serviced in that year, Eg, if firm has to pay up loans/EMI of Rs 1 Cr in the year, and profits generated is 2 Cr then DSCR is 2. DSCR > 1.25 is considered healthy.

What is Quick Ratio? Why is it important?
Quick Ratio by formula is (Current Assets Inventory)/Current Liabilities, Current by definition means ability to convert to cash within 1 year or lesser, the ratio reflects liquid working capital of any business, and a quick ratio >1 shows that any point of time the party has More Liquid assets (Inc. Market Credit) to meet off his current liabilities.
Why is insurance important in the financial assessment?
Insurance ensures that any future risks are secured and hence adds to the stability position for businesses for any future unforeseen events.
How do I find the liquidity position of the business?
Critical parameters to assess the liquidity position of the dealer are Current ratio, Quick Ratio, interest coverage ratio, Banking to TO and DSCR.
What is Bureau Score? Why is it important?
Bureau score is nothing but a score assigned by external parties on the credit worthiness of parties. There are multiple agencies, all being authorized by RBI to carry out this assessment, CIBIL (the most popular) is one of them, a score > 750 is considered healthy and a score below 600 is considered risky.
